Existence

01 / THE WAKE-UP CALL

At the moment, there are many labs working in the advancement of artificial intelligence and many with the focus of AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) and ASI (Artificial Super Intelligence). Organisations such as OpenAI, DeepMind, LAION and SSI are working on critical and large-scale projects that are valuable but often overlook research pivots and directions that are either **too publicly heated/financially and marketing stand-point won't sell.**

Sleeping AI was meant to change that narrative and work on research questions that are heated and tough so that the general public have someone afterall who focus on **raw research** rather than weighing benefits.

We commit large-enough resources on our projects depending on how much we have available. We are inspired by "the Anonymous" team who first formed a decentralised intelligence network of talented programmers and curious folks with nothing but shared interest.

CRITERIA FOR PROJECT SELECTION

  • there are some well-known existing folks working on this question.
  • the field according to present outcome has a viable future.
  • collection of dataset and training is legally and financially challenging.
  • it's simply fun!
